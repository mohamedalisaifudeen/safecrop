About SafeCrop

Protecting Crops, Preserving Wildlife

Who We Are

SafeCrop is an innovative project designed to tackle the challenge of human-elephant conflict in Sri Lanka. By leveraging IoT technology, we provide sustainable solutions to protect crops, preserve biodiversity, and empower local communities.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with advanced tools for crop protection while fostering coexistence between humans and elephants.

Our Vision

To build a future where human-wildlife conflicts are resolved through innovation and collaboration.

How It All Began

SafeCrop was founded to address the growing problem of crop damage caused by wild elephants in Sri Lanka. Combining technology with empathy, we've created a system that ensures sustainable protection for farmers while safeguarding wildlife.

Our Values

Sustainability

Eco-friendly, long-lasting solutions.

Innovation

Harnessing technology to solve real-world problems.

Collaboration

Working with communities and experts for holistic solutions.

Compassion

Caring for farmers, wildlife, and the environment.
