About Us

We Are SafeCrop

SafeCrop is an innovative project designed to tackle the challenge of human-elephant conflict in Sri Lanka. By leveraging IoT technology, we provide sustainable solutions to protect crops, preserve biodiversity, and empower local communities.

Read More

Why Choose Us

Cutting-Edge Technology

SafeCrop utilizes advanced IoT technology to provide real-time alerts, location tracking, and automated systems to protect your fields from elephant damage. By combining technology with practical solutions, we offer farmers and wildlife officers a smarter way to safeguard crops.

Real-Time Monitoring and Alerts

Unlike traditional methods, SafeCrop offers instant alerts when elephants are near your field, ensuring that you can take timely action. Whether it's triggering an alarm or notifying wildlife officers, you'll always be in the loop with real-time updates.

Cost-Effective and Preventative

Rather than relying on costly repairs and post-damage recovery, SafeCrop helps you prevent damage before it happens. With features like alarm systems, real-time tracking, and automated alerts, you can save both time and money.

Seamless Communication

SafeCrop ensures that you never miss an important alert. With automated calls and text messages, the system communicates directly with both farmers and wildlife officers, making sure they are always informed and prepared for action.
